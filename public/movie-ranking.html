The Best Movies According to Csilla

My top 3 movies of all-time.

The Visitors

A medieval knight and his servant ask a familiar wizard to move them back in time to prevent father-in-law's accidentally killing. Instead, they fly away to the 20th century.

Taxi

To work off his tarnished driving record, a hip taxi driver must chauffeur a loser police inspector on the trail of German bank robbers

Asterix & Obelix: Mission Cleopatra

Cleopatra, queen of Egypt, believes she can build a temple in record time and makes a bet with Julius Caesar.
